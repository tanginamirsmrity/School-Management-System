The Inter-Community School Dhaka (ICS) is a fully accredited and renowned international school in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We are the only school in Dhaka offering the International Baccalaureate (IB) Programme for Primary Years, Middle Years, and Diploma Studies.
As an IB World School established in 1982, ICS provides personalised education for students aged 5 to 18 years, including a bilingual diploma option. Our supportive community and rigorous curriculum prepares students to flourish in today's quickly changing world.
